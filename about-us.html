A note from our founder

Quarantine Airways was founded in 2020, at the height of the COVID-19 crisis. We found ourselves in an unprecedented state of motionlessness. No one was traveling, for business or for pleasure, and we all had to stay home for the collective health of the world. And it was the right thing to do. But I really missed the excitement of traveling: the feeling of escaping the bubble of your home, of learning something new about the world we share, of being in a phase of transit.

Thus, Quarantine Airways was born: a virtual, interactive air travel experience delivered through the magic of Twitter DMs. It’s by no means a perfect simulation of what it’s like to fly, but I hope it’s something fun you can enjoy whenever you feel the need to escape for a bit.

It’s just a tap away.

Brian Yung
Founder, CEO, COO, CMO, Designer, Copywriter,
Customer Service & Social Media Intern
