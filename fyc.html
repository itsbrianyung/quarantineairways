The project

In 2020, we find ourselves in an unprecedented state of motionlessness. I miss the excitement of traveling — the feeling of escaping the bubble of your home. Thus, Quarantine Airways was born.

Quarantine Airways is a virtual airline experience delivered through a Twitter chatbot. I sought to recreate the air travel experience in a digital context: getting your boarding pass, watching movies set in the city you’re visiting, learning facts about your destination, and more. I taught myself how to code a Twitter bot from scratch, wrote the dialogue, and consulted airline industry professionals to make sure it all feels authentic.

The campaign

Fact: I’d always wanted to run my own marketing campaign. With Quarantine Airways being the perfect opportunity, I set out to plan and execute a multichannel launch campaign that was tonally consistent and visually cohesive.
