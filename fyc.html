About this project

In 2020, we are finding ourselves in an unprecedented state of motionlessness. I miss the excitement of traveling — the feeling of escaping the bubble of your home.

Quarantine Airways is a virtual airline experience delivered through a Twitter chatbot. I sought to recreate the air travel experience in a digital context. Getting your boarding pass. Watching movies set in the city you’re visiting. Learning facts about your destination + more. I taught myself how to code a Twitter bot from scratch, wrote the dialogue, and consulted airline industry professionals to make sure it all feels authentic. Created a visual identity for the brand, too.

This was also the perfect opportunity for me to plan and run a marketing campaign. The multichannel launch campaign includes:
- A website (https://quarantineairways.com)
- An Instagram content strategy (https://instagram.com/flywithQA/)
- YouTube and Twitter ads
- Digital out-of-home billboards near major transit hubs
Fun fact: it is surprisingly cheap to advertise in Times Square, NYC (!).

My hope is for this to be something fun you can enjoy whenever you feel the need to escape for a bit.
