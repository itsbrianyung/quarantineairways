Redefining domestic travel

The entire world has been brought to a standstill. As we all stay home to help contain the spread of COVID-19, planes are no longer taking to the skies, passengers are no longer filling airport security lines, and miles are no longer being earned on the travel credit card you’re paying a ridiculous annual fee for. Air travel seems to have become a thing of the distant past.

But what if there’s a way to recreate the experience of traveling, right from the comfort of your own home? Introducing: Quarantine Airways.
